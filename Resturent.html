    ABOUT BARISTA


    Our journey from the bean to the cup started in year 2000. Café culture had not set its footprint and coffee was still considered a luxury.

    Our quest to provide a truly international experience and brew a truly Italian cuppa, led us to create the brand Barista.

    • No one has mastered coffee better than the Italians
    • Tipping our hats their way, Barista brings new meaning to Italian coffee and eats in the Indian sub-continent.

    Today, we continue to brew coffee the way world does and in our journey we have created coffee relationships with our customers across India, Sri Lanka, Maldives, Bangladesh, Nepal and Myanmar.
